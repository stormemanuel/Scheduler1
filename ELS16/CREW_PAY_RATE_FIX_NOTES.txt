Crew Pay Rate Fix

This build separates crew payout rates from client billing rates on the Crew page.

What changed:
- Added shared crew payout defaults in lib/crew-pay-defaults.ts.
- Crew CSV import now fills missing pay rates from ELS payout defaults instead of leaving them at $0.
- Editing a crew role auto-fills the pay rate when the rate is blank/zero.
- Crew detail tab now labels these as crew payout rates, not client billing rates.
- Added supabase/fix_crew_pay_rates.sql to correct existing crew_positions rows that were imported with client billing prices such as $700, $650, $550, and $500.

Important:
Run supabase/fix_crew_pay_rates.sql one time in Supabase SQL Editor if your existing Crew page already shows billing rates as worker pay rates.
